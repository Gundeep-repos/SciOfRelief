Sci of Relief


This website is an online portal for various types of counselling offered by the university. The next tab will lead to a series of questions which can be broadly classified as

  • Stress Level
    • An estimate of how would you rate your stress level
  • Life Problems
    • These questions pertain to housing,financial,legal isssues and any kind of harrasment or problem with another person
  • Academics
    • Academic problems include stress related to current course,grade issues or any other university related problems
  • Mental Health
    • These questions are related to a person's emotional well being such as mood,sleeping and heating habits or alcohol related issues
